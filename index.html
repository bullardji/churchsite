WELCOME

Our Savior Lutheran Church is a member congregation of The Lutheran Church—Missouri Synod. We believe, teach, and confess the faith as it is taught by the Holy Scriptures and faithfully confessed in the Book of Concord. We are committed to the pure preaching of God's Word and the right administration of the holy Sacraments, to the catechesis of all people in the Christian faith, and to liturgical worship consistent with our Reformation heritage.
